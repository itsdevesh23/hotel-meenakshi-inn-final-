About Us

Our story, mission, and values

Our Story

Founded in 1995, Meenakshi Inn has been a symbol of luxury and hospitality in the heart of the city. What started as a small family-owned restaurant has evolved into a premier hotel and dining destination.

Our commitment to excellence, attention to detail, and passion for creating memorable experiences have earned us a reputation attention to detail, and passion for creating memorable experiences have earned us a reputation as one of the finest establishments in the region.

Today, Meenakshi Inn continues to uphold its tradition of hospitality while embracing modern amenities and innovative culinary techniques to provide our guests with an unforgettable experience.

Quality

We use only the finest ingredients and materials to ensure exceptional quality in everything we offer.

Service

Our dedicated staff is committed to providing attentive and personalized service to every guest.

Tradition

We honor traditional recipes and hospitality practices while incorporating modern innovations.
